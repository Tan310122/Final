What is a free tour?

The Free Tour is a walking tour with no need for public transport and is designed for independent travellers and small groups. Free Walking Tour is a great and budget-friendly way to explore the city in depth, learn about its people, culture & heritage and to make friends with other travellers. Simply book your spot in advance online & show up at the meeting point on the day. There is no payment necessary at the start of the tour but you are welcome to tip your guide at the end!

Why join HaNoi day tour's free tours?

  • Covers many of HaNoi's major landmarks & attractions
  • Meeting points are conveniently located at train stations
  • No upfront payment! Tipping for tour guides for a job well done
  • Suitable for everyone. Tours run rain or shine
